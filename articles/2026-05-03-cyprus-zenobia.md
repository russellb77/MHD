A week in Cyprus in early May saw a group of Mid Herts Divers heading for warmer waters, wrecks, and a healthy dose of Mediterranean sunshine. For many, this would be a first visit to the famous Zenobia wreck.

The Zenobia is a Swedish roll-on roll-off ferry that sank in 1980 just outside Larnaca harbour. At 172m long, it's one of the top 10 wreck dives in the world.
