# Privacy Policy

Mid Herts Divers takes your privacy seriously. This page explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

## What We Collect

When you fill in a form on our website, we collect:
- Your name
- Email address
- Phone number (if provided)
- Any message content you provide

## How We Use It

We use your information to:
- Respond to your enquiries
- Book and manage try dive sessions
- Send relevant club communications (only if you join)

## Data Storage

Form submissions are processed by Netlify and forwarded to our club email. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

## Cookies

This website uses minimal cookies for basic functionality. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.

## Your Rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing hello@midhertsdivers.com.

## Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at hello@midhertsdivers.com.
