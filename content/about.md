# About Mid Herts Divers

We are British Sub Aqua Club (BSAC) branch 1784, based at the Hatfield Swim Centre in Hertfordshire.

## Who We Are

Mid Herts Divers is a friendly scuba diving and snorkelling club that welcomes both complete beginners and experienced divers. We train to BSAC standards, which are internationally recognised.

## What We Do

- **Pool sessions** every Thursday evening at Hatfield Swim Centre
- **Open water training** at local dive sites
- **UK dive trips** to popular locations including Portland, Plymouth, and Scotland
- **Overseas trips** to destinations like Cyprus, the Red Sea, and beyond
- **Social events** because diving is about the people as much as the water

## Training

Our nationally qualified instructors offer training from complete beginner (Ocean Diver) through to advanced levels. All equipment is provided during training.

## Join Us

Whether you're curious about trying scuba for the first time or an experienced diver looking for a club, we'd love to hear from you. Book a try dive or get in touch!
