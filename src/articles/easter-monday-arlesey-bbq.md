The first bank holiday of the year gave us an opportunity to have an extended diving session at Arlesey, and at a more social hour too – perfect timing to combine with having a post-dive BBQ.

We had 10 divers in the water taking advantage of the exceptional vis, and along with others who came along only for the social side of things, about 16 people were well-fed with good quality burgers and hotdogs expertly cooked on a braie by our wonderful chef for the day – Tracey.

With lovely sunshine, a light breeze, and clear skies, and two large swans for company as well as a couple of pike – it sure was a wonderful way to spend an Easter Monday. It's something we intend to repeat next month.
