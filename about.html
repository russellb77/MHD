About Us

BSAC Branch 1784 — Diving since the 1970s

Who We Are

Mid Herts Divers is a friendly scuba diving and snorkelling club that welcomes both complete beginners and experienced divers. We are British Sub Aqua Club (BSAC) branch 1784, based at the Hatfield Swim Centre in Hertfordshire.

What We Do

  • 🏊 Pool sessions every Thursday evening at Hatfield Swim Centre
  • 🌊 Open water training at local dive sites
  • 🇬🇧 UK dive trips to Portland, Plymouth, Scotland, and more
  • ✈️ Overseas trips to Cyprus, the Red Sea, and beyond
  • 🎉 Social events because diving is about the people as much as the water

Training

Our nationally qualified instructors offer training from complete beginner (Ocean Diver) through to advanced levels. We train to BSAC standards, which are internationally recognised. All equipment is provided during training.

Whether you're curious about trying scuba for the first time or an experienced diver looking for a club, we'd love to hear from you.
