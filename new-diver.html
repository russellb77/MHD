New to Diving

If you are new to scuba diving and want to learn you are in the right place! Learning to scuba dive can be a life-changing experience, bringing a whole host of exciting underwater opportunities your way.

Our team of friendly instructors will teach you to become a safe, confident and self-reliant diver in a period of weeks or months. By the end of your scuba lessons, you will have unlocked the door to a new underwater world and countless new adventures.

The BSAC Journey

Your journey will start with the Ocean Diver course, which is the entry-level open water diver qualification. This will equip you with the core skills to dive to a maximum of 20 metres in open water, in the UK and abroad.

BSAC diving skills are taught progressively, with each new skill and experience built upon, step-by-step. The blend of theory, swimming pool and open water skills sessions along with continual practice enables you to develop your diving in a safe and enjoyable way.

Diver Grades

With BSAC there are five internationally-recognised diver grades, from the entry-level Ocean Diver through to First Class Diver. Additionally, there are many skills courses (known as Skill Development Courses or SDCs) to cater for specific interests such as wreck diving, safety and rescue, photography, seamanship, technical diving or boat handling.

Come and see us!

If you are interested in meeting us to find out more, please come along to the Hatfield pool on a Wednesday and check us out. You will see what a typical evening of training looks like and ask any questions you may have. The club meets from around 8:30 pm till 10:30 pm every week.

Or if you are ready to dive in, please complete the try dive form and we will get you booked in to start your adventure!
