Book a Try Dive

Experience the thrill of breathing underwater for the very first time

What to Expect

In the safe confines of the pool, one of our friendly BSAC Instructors will introduce you to the basic skills and equipment needed to experience scuba diving.

Details

  • 📍 Hatfield Swim Centre
  • ⏱️ Approximately 2 hours
  • 🤿 All equipment provided
  • 👥 Small groups (max 4 people)
  • 🎂 Minimum age: 12 years
  • 💰 £25 per person

Requirements

You should be comfortable in water and able to swim 200m. You'll need to complete a medical self-declaration form — we'll send this to you after booking.
