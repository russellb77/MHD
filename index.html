Mid Herts Divers

Scuba Diving and Snorkelling Club

We are British Sub Aqua Club branch 1784, based at the Hatfield Swim Centre. We welcome both trainee and experienced divers and snorkellers. Come dive with us!

New to Diving?

If you are new to scuba diving and want to learn you are in the right place! Learning to scuba dive can be a life-changing experience, bringing a whole host of exciting underwater opportunities your way.

Already a Diver?

Are you a qualified scuba diver with PADI, SSI, RAID, GUE or another agency? Great! Continue the adventure… join great people in your area on some memory-making scuba diving.

Get back in the water »

Try Scuba

In the safe confines of the pool, one of our friendly BSAC Instructors will introduce you to the basic skills and equipment needed to experience the thrill of breathing underwater for the very first time.
